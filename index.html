About Us

Charity: Water is on a quest to provide clean and safe drinking water to every individual occupying the planet.

We fund and implement sustainable water solutions in the world's most distant and underprivileged communities by combining radical transparency, innovative fundraising, and dignity-first storytelling.

However, we cannot accomplish our mission alone. People like you are the driving force behind our work.

Every supporter, from monthly donations to college students, gamers to marathon runners, contributes to the creation of pure water through compassion.

We collaborate with experienced local organizations and engineers to construct community-owned, sustainable water projects across the globe.
